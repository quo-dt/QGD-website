Who is Rina?

Tennoji Rina is a character in the "Love Live!" series. She is a first-year at Nijigasaki High School. She has trouble conveying her emotions through facial expressions, leading her to be seen as unfriendly since childhood. Spending most of her time alone, she developed interest in technology and became a tech-savvy.

Eventually, she figured she could draw faces on a notebook to express her feelings which she calls the "Rina-chan board", followed by the invention of an electronic mask that changes expressions automatically. She wears it on live performances and activities that require both hands.

Chasing a Dream

In search of a club to join as mandated by her school, Rina was thrilled by the school idols' performance. She realized she could express her emotions by performing on stage, showing everyone who she truly is. With the support of her best friend Ai, the two joined the school idol club together.

It wasn't all smooth sailing as she didn't have the electronic Rina-chan board yet. The day before her scheduled debut performance, she looked at herself in a mirror, noticing the face that still couldn't put on a smile. She hid herself in a cardboard box out of frustration. Owing to her friends' support, she got to her senses and invented the board she could wear on stage to connect with the audience.
